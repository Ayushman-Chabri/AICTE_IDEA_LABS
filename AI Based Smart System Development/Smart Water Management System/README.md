**Project Title**
Smart Water Management System

**Problem Statement**
The aim of the project is to develop a smart water management system using ESP32 Microcontroller and a RGB LED and Servo Motor.
Based on the water levels inside the tank(simulated artificially using the input of a potentiometer) the LED must produce light signals to indicate the status of the tank and the fins of the servo motor must rotate to simulate the functioning of the water valve.

**Specifications**
*Water Level*-: 0 to 33%
*Tank Status*-: Low Level
*RGB LED*-: Red
*Servo valve*-: Fully Open

*Water Level*-: 34 to 66%
*Tank Status*-: Medium Level
*RGB LED*-: Yellow
*Servo valve*-: Half Open

*Water Level*-: 67 to 100%
*Tank Status*-: Full Tank
*RGB LED*-: Green
*Servo valve*-: Closed

**How to operate?**
The simulation can be operated on the Wokwi Simulator. The user needs rotate the dial of the potentiometer to simulate the input of the sensor and accordingly the rest of the system would respond in adherance to the specifications as listed above.
